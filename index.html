Another Way

by Jing Wang

This is an attempt to build a spiritual space in A Tactile, Auditory Environment.

How can we read without eyes? What kind of a world would it be? The aim of this work is to build a spiritual space in a 'tactile, auditory' environment, to explore a 'sightless' spiritual space. I seek to amplify the senses of hearing and touch and diminish the sense of sight. In this work, touch is the trigger for vibrations and sounds to obtain information.
